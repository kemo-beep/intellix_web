## Brand & design (apply site-wide)

| Element | Direction |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Name** | Intellix |
| **Positioning** | Smart, practical, career-focused digital skills — not theory-only training |
| **Primary tagline** | “Unlock Your Digital Genius” |
| **Secondary taglines** | “Learn. Create. Lead.” · “Smart Skills. Real Impact.” |
| **Logo** | Minimal “I” with circuit lines, or brain/node motif; geometric, flat |
| **Colors** | Deep Blue `#1E40AF`, Electric Teal `#14B8A6`, accent Bright Yellow `#FACC15`, neutrals white/gray |
| **Typography** | Rajdhani (headings), Poppins (body) — or Montserrat as alternate geometric sans |
| **Domains to check** | `intellix.io`, `intellix.academy`, `getintellix.com` (and `.com` if available) |

**Hero copy (long form, for A/B testing):**  
“Unlock your digital genius. Learn modern skills that help you get hired, win freelance work, and grow your career — with real projects and portfolio-ready outcomes.”

**Social / ad hook:**  
“Turn your creativity into income. Master graphic design, video, **photography**, **videography**, and **software development** with Intellix — practical training built for jobs and freelancing.”

## Global UX & conversion

- **Sticky header:** Home · Why Intellix · Courses & Programs · Portfolio · Enroll · Blog · Contact
- **Primary CTAs:** “Enroll now” / “Join free intro” (see **free intro funnel** below)
- **Secondary CTA (Gambia):** Prominent **WhatsApp** — primary channel for questions and bookings
- **Footer / mega-menu:** Multi-column catalog like major learning sites — see [`pages/site-navigation.md`](pages/site-navigation.md) (Company, Resources, Plans & community, **Subjects**, **Languages & tools**, Career building). Nav links, social handles, WhatsApp, optional newsletter, corporate line.
- **Free intro funnel:** “Free intro class” → capture lead → convert to paid cohorts (mirror offline flyers + school-adjacent marketing)
