# LensLab — photography

**Program:** LensLab · **Parent:** [Courses overview](courses.md)

**Photography** as its own program (camera + light + composition + post-processing stills). Not the same as graphic design; bridges naturally to **EditPro** and **SceneCraft**.

## Tracks

| Track | Focus | Example outcomes | **Tools you’ll learn & use** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Foundations** | Exposure triangle, RAW, lenses, composition, basic Lightroom | Controlled exercise set + “best 10” portfolio | **Adobe Lightroom Classic** (or **Lightroom** cloud) + **Adobe Camera Raw**; **Photoshop** for retouch basics; school **DSLR/mirrorless** or approved smartphone RAW |
| **Portrait & people** | Natural/flash, posing, skin tones, environmental portrait | Small portrait series (studio or location) | Lightroom + Photoshop (**frequency separation** / light retouch as you teach); **off-camera flash** or LED if available |
| **Product & commercial** | Tabletop, lighting setups, catalog-style frames | Product set for a local brand or spec brief | Tripod, **light tent** or softboxes, Lightroom/Photoshop; optional **Capture One** if you standardize on it |
| **Events & lifestyle** | Events, candids, storytelling sequences | Event or documentary-style mini essay | Fast cards + backup workflow; Lightroom culling; **Photo Mechanic** optional for speed |

## Hardware (lab / loan / demo)

School **mirrorless or DSLR** bodies, **50mm / versatile zoom** lenses, **tripods**, **reflectors**, LED or flash kits as budget allows. State **BYOD** rules and minimum body/lens if students bring gear.

## Optional crossover

**Canva** or **Photoshop** for quick social crops from LensLab shoots — links to **[Pixel Mastery](courses-pixel-mastery.md)**.
