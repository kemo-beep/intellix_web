# SceneCraft — videography (production)

**Program:** SceneCraft · **Parent:** [Courses overview](courses.md)

**Videography** = **getting the image and sound in-camera** (and basic on-set workflow): framing, movement, lighting, audio, planning. **EditPro** completes the pipeline in post.

## Tracks

| Track | Focus | Example outcomes | **Tools you’ll learn & use** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Production foundations** | Camera settings for video, shot types, continuity, basic audio | Shot-matching exercise + short silent sequence | **Mirrorless/DSLR** video modes, **smartphone** cinema apps; **DJI Osmo Pocket 3** (gimbal camera, ActiveTrack, Creator Combo accessories if available); **DJI Mimo** app; basic **shot list** + **clapper** or sync tricks |
| **Content creator production** | Self-shooting, talking head, B-roll, mobile + mirrorless | Creator-style package of usable rushes for EditPro | **Osmo Pocket 3** or phone + **gimbal**; **wireless lav** (e.g. **Rode Wireless** family) or on-camera mic; natural light + **LED** key |
| **Commercial & corporate** | Interviews, b-roll for business, simple lighting setups | Corporate spec spot **footage** + shot list | **Two-camera** interview setup where possible; **tripod** + **monopod**; **three-point lighting** basics; **XLR or 3.5mm** into camera or recorder |
| **Cinematic & storytelling** | Scene coverage, mood, movement (gimbal/slider as available) | Short narrative or branded mini-film **production** | **Gimbal** (Osmo Pocket 3 / phone / mirrorless rig), **slider** if available; lens choice and **ND** filters for outdoor exposure |

## Hardware inventory

Publish what you actually have: e.g. **DJI Osmo Pocket 3**, **mirrorless cameras**, **shotgun + wireless mics**, **LED panels**, **tripods/monopods**, **memory cards & readers**. **Drones** only if licensed and legal for your jurisdiction — omit from public list until compliant.

## Software on set

**DJI Mimo**; camera vendor apps if useful; **Blackmagic Camera** (iPhone) optional; data offload and **proxy** workflow can preview **DaVinci Resolve** or **Premiere** ingest (ties to **EditPro**).
