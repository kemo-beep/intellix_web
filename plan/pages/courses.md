## Suggested Gambia pricing bands

- Shorter / basic modules: roughly **GMD 3,000–8,000**
- Deeper / advanced tracks: roughly **GMD 10,000–25,000**
- Optional **monthly access** model: **GMD 1,000+** if you offer it
- Call out **payment plans** and **student discounts** on [Enroll](enroll.md)
