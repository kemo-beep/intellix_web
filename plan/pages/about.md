# About us

**Purpose:** Trust, mission, and **local roots** without overselling compliance you have not yet secured.

## Suggested copy blocks

- **Mission:** Empower people with **practical** digital skills for employment and self-employment.
- **Vision:** Anyone can turn creativity and tech skills into a sustainable livelihood.
- **Where we are:** The Gambia — mention **accessible location** (e.g. Serrekunda / Kanifing / Banjul area when fixed) and easy transport where true.
- **Approach:** Learning by doing, portfolio-first, freelancing guidance; optional **corporate training** and **online** offerings as you scale.
- **Team:** Lead instructor(s), admin; room for **career / placement** role later.
- **Certificates (honest):** If you pursue an **education license** and recognized assessment, state that certificates align with regulatory requirements; if still in process, say “working toward” or focus on **portfolio and skills** until licensed.
