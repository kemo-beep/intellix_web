# EditPro — video editing

**Program:** EditPro · **Parent:** [Courses overview](courses.md)

**EditPro** is **post-production** (timeline, color, sound, export). Pair with **[SceneCraft](courses-scenecraft.md)** for students who need **shooting** skills first.

## Tracks

| Track | Focus | Example outcomes | **Tools you’ll learn & use** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Content creator / social video** | Short-form, hooks, captions, vertical, platform-native pacing | Batch of reels/shorts + consistent style | **Adobe Premiere Pro**; **CapCut** (desktop/mobile); captions and vertical workflows; optional **After Effects** for simple motion |
| **Commercial & promotional** | Ads, promos, product spots, client-style revisions | 30–60s spec commercial or promo cut | **Premiere Pro**; **Blackmagic DaVinci Resolve** (cut + basic color); **After Effects** if you teach titles/simple VFX |
| **YouTube & long-form** | Structure, B-roll, pacing, chapters, basic motion titles | 8–15 min “channel-ready” episode or doc-style piece | **Premiere Pro** or **Apple Final Cut Pro** (Mac lab); chapter markers; audio cleanup in **Audition** or **Fairlight** (DaVinci) |
| **Corporate & events** | Highlights, interviews, simple multicam, clean audio | Event highlight or interview package | **Premiere Pro** or **Final Cut Pro**; **DaVinci Resolve** for multicam/color; **Adobe Audition** or **Fairlight** for dialogue |

## Program note

Standardize **one primary NLE per cohort** where possible (e.g. **Premiere Pro** + **DaVinci Resolve** free tier); offer **Final Cut Pro** on **Mac** stations. **CapCut** is ideal for creator-speed modules. Mention **Media Encoder** / export presets for delivery specs.
